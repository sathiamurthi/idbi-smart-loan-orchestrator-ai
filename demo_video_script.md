# IDBI Smart Loan Orchestrator AI (ISLO-AI)
## 3-Minute Demo Video Recording Script & Walkthrough

This guide provides a professional, step-by-step blueprint to record your 3-minute hackathon demo video. 

* **Target Length**: 180 seconds (3 Minutes)
* **Pre-requisites**: Open your live Vercel URL: **[https://idbi-smart-loan-orchestrator-ai.vercel.app](https://idbi-smart-loan-orchestrator-ai.vercel.app)** in your browser. Download the sample test documents from `public/documents/` (or from the dashboard links) to your desktop first.

---

### ⏱️ Part 1: Introduction & The Problem Statement
* **Duration**: 0:00 – 0:30 (30 seconds)
* **Screen to Show**: Main dashboard home view (**Customer Portal** tab).
* **Action**: Hover over the expandable **Platform Vision & Problem Statement** card at the top, explaining the legacy silos.
* **Voiceover Script**:
  > *"Hello, we are team OrchAI, presenting the IDBI Lending Orchestration Control Panel. Traditional banking loan processing is slowed down by fragmented legacy databases. Applications pass through multiple departments—Relationship Managers, Credit Officers, and Risk Analysts—in a slow, linear queue.*
  >
  > *Our solution is an AI Operating System for lending. By combining policy-driven orchestration, transaction narration mining, and explainable AI, we compress loan processing SLAs from 5 days down to under 5 minutes."*

---

### ⏱️ Part 2: Customer Ingestion & Document Verification
* **Duration**: 0:30 – 1:15 (45 seconds)
* **Screen to Show**: Customer Portal form panel.
* **Action**:
  1. Fill out the application form on the left: Name: `Test Applicant`, Employer: `Tata Consultancy Services`, Salary: `150,000`, CIBIL: `810`, Loan Amount: `800,000`.
  2. Click the file upload box under **Attach Verification Documents** and upload the pre-downloaded `test_salary_slip.pdf` and `test_bank_statement.pdf`. Show the upload progress indicator.
  3. Click **Orchestrate Loan Process**.
* **Voiceover Script**:
  > *"Let's look at the customer journey. When an applicant submits a loan file, they upload verification PDFs directly. Our upload portal handles simulated OCR extraction and compliance file validation.*
  >
  > *When we click 'Orchestrate', the Coordinator Agent reads the bank's lending policies in real-time, builds a custom pipeline of analytical agents on-the-fly, and launches the execution flow."*

---

### ⏱️ Part 3: AI Orchestration Hub (The Digital Twin)
* **Duration**: 1:15 – 2:00 (45 seconds)
* **Screen to Show**: **AI Orchestration Hub** tab.
* **Action**:
  1. Show the visual node flow executing step-by-step.
  2. Click on the completed **Bank Statement Agent** node.
  3. Point to the **Identified Active Liabilities** card in the output inspector.
* **Voiceover Script**:
  > *"Welcome to the AI Orchestration Hub—the Digital Twin of the lending workflow. Here, you can watch the agents process the file in real-time. Our OCR agent extracts credentials, while the Salary agent calculates stability metrics.*
  >
  > *Crucially, our Bank Statement Agent scans transaction narrations to automatically identify outstanding loans. Here, it parsed the narration tags and isolated active Home and Personal Loan EMIs, deducting them from the applicant's surplus headroom."*

---

### ⏱️ Part 4: Human-in-the-Loop & Decision Overrides
* **Duration**: 2:00 – 2:40 (40 seconds)
* **Screen to Show**: **Manager Desk** tab.
* **Action**:
  1. Select **Priya Sharma (APP_1002)** which is pending review (HOLD).
  2. Show the **Explainable AI (XAI)** decision card details.
  3. Move the approved loan slider to override the amount, adjust the interest rate, add a note: `"Approved with rate adjustment based on TCS employment"`, and click **Approve Application**.
* **Voiceover Script**:
  > *"When an application crosses a high-value boundary, the Coordinator places it on HOLD and escalates it to the Manager Desk. Here, senior managers audit the Explainable AI summaries.*
  >
  > *The manager can override the approved loan amount or interest APR, add audit comments, and securely sign off manually. This keeps human-in-the-loop oversight perfectly integrated."*

---

### ⏱️ Part 5: Conclusion & Future Vision
* **Duration**: 2:40 – 3:00 (20 seconds)
* **Screen to Show**: **Pitch Deck** tab (Slide 10: Future Roadmap) or Vercel main page.
* **Action**: Scroll to the Pitch Deck tab, displaying the final roadmap slides.
* **Voiceover Script**:
  > *"Our system is lightweight, highly portable, and runs entirely in the browser. In the next phase, we will integrate with IDBI Sandbox APIs and deploy Relationship Manager mobile companions.*
  >
  > *We are transforming fragmented lending workflows into a unified, transparent, and explainable decision network. Thank you!"*
